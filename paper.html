📄 Paper Bot

Two virtual $1,000 portfolios trade our signals once a day (03:10 UTC, GitHub Actions). Conservative: accumulation signals only (hidden buyer, holder surge, accumulation divergence, liquidity inflow) + quality filters (TVL≥$50K, holders≥5K, liq/mcap≥2%), +15%/−7%/5d. Aggressive: adds momentum, breakout, dip-reversal, flow imbalance; looser filters, +30%/−12%/3d. Both pay real costs (CPMM slippage + 0.3%/leg) and force-exit on rug (TVL −25%/day). The edge table decides what a real bot would trade. Alt (CEX): a separate $1,000 portfolio on non-TON majors (HYPE, ZEC) via CoinGecko — price/volume signals only (breakout, vol spike, momentum, dip reversal), 0.1%/leg, +20%/−10%/5d; no on-chain signals since CEX coins have no DEX metrics.
